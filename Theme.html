Theme

Name a major theme and say how it’s being developed, including one typed and cited quote that addresses this theme with your explanation for how it is deepened and how it’s relevant to us and our times.

Posted by Jackie on January 31, 2023

Theme:Pain vs Death

One major theme that was developed in this chapter is the idea of putting someone out of pain by killing them.


In page 353, it states:

"Finally they reached the edge of the park. Snowman had to shoot only two more disintegrating people. He was doing them a favour, so he didn’t feel too bad about it. He felt worse about other things." (353)

This quote depicts the idea of killing someone in order to end suffering. This theme has came up multiple times in story like when Crake ask Jimmy question about whether he would kill someone to spare them pain. Also, it explores ideas if you should make up a story to spare the pain of someone like how Snowman spares the Crakers pain through stories.
