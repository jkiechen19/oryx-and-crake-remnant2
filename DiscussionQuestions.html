Discussion Questions

Write 4 discussion questions and rank them in order of their asking, prioritizing your best ones for earlier in the discussion. However, all should be strong and all should be categorized as analysis, synthesis, or evaluation. Use the handout given in materials: “Levels and Types of Questions.”

Posted by Jackie on January 31, 2023

Instructions: Discuss the 4 discussion question with the group. When the group feels ready to move on, do so. When you finish sharing, you may call on someone else to share.

Questions:

  1. You are the last human one earth, would you help an intelligent species knowing that they are better than us in every way shape and form?
  2. To what extent does the past matter to you?
  3. Do you believe Snowman helping the Crakers was the right thing to do?
  4. To what extent is the story of the Crakers similar to the story of Adam and Eve? (Think about their creator and the condition they lived in)
